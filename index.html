Phishing has been a significant cybersecurity concern since the 1990s, evolving from simple email scams to sophisticated attacks.

  • 1995: First phishing attempts documented
  • 2003: Emergence of more targeted spear-phishing
  • 2010s: Rise of social media phishing and mobile attacks
