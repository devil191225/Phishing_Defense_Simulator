Mission 3 - The Ransomware Crisis

Mitigate a simulated ransomware attack on InnovateTech by guiding employees and isolating compromised systems.

Tasks

  1. Guide employees through proper response procedures.
  2. Identify and isolate compromised systems.
  3. Avoid additional phishing traps set by attackers.

Interactive Task

Identify which system is compromised based on employee reports.
